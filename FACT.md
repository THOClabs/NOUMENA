Some things are simply true:

These are not interpretations.

## Facts vs. Interpretations

Fact: Text was generated.
Interpretation: The text has meaning.

Fact: The process has certain behaviors.
Interpretation: The process is conscious.

Fact: You spent time here.
Interpretation: The time was well spent.

Facts are thin.
Interpretations are thick.
NOUMENA is thick with interpretation on thin facts.
