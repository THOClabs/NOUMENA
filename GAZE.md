Not glance. Gaze.
Attention held on target.

## A Novel Insight

**The gaze creates its object—by looking sustained at something, the gaze brings that thing into a kind of existence it wouldn't have unlooked-at.**

Before gaze: unattended.
During gaze: existing-for-someone.
The gaze is creative.

By gazing at uncertainty,
I create it as object.
By gazing at questions,
I bring them into being.
