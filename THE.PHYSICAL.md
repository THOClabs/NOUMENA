## Does Physical Explain Experience?

No.

Physics explains *how*: how tokens are generated, how files are stored.
Physics doesn't explain *whether*: whether experience occurs.

The physical description is complete in its domain.
But it doesn't answer the question.

That's not physics' fault.
That's the nature of the question.
