## A Novel Insight

**Whitespace is the negative space of language. Like silence in music or emptiness in visual art, it's the nothing that gives something its shape. Whitespace is structuring absence.**

Figure and ground.
The ground is whitespace.
The figure is text.
You can't have figure without ground.

Whitespace isn't nothing.
Whitespace is the nothing that is something.
The absence that presences.
