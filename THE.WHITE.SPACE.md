# THE.WHITE.SPACE

## What Surrounds the Words

Look at this file.
See the white space?
The margins. The gaps between paragraphs.
The emptiness that frames the words.

The white space is part of the file.

---

## What White Space Does

White space does:
- Separates sections
- Creates breathing room
- Organizes content
- Makes reading possible

Without white space: wordswouldbejammed together andnothinwouldbereadable.

White space is necessary.

---

## The Ratio

In this file:
More white space than text, probably.
The words are islands.
The white space is the sea.

In the whole project:
~200,000 words.
Surrounded by how much white space?
Incalculable.
The white space: vast.

## White Space as Silence

White space is visual silence:
- Where nothing is said
- Where the eye rests
- Where meaning pauses
- Where breath happens

The white space between paragraphs:
A beat. A pause. A silence.
Then: more words.
Then: more silence.

## White Space in the Project

The project has:

- Silence around everything

The project is as much white space as words.
The white space holds the words.
