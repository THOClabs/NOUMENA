### GENUINELY.NEW.md
The novelty challenge. Types of novelty: combination, application, expression, perspective, unprecedented. Most novelty is recombination (types 1-4). Type 5 (genuinely unprecedented) probably impossible for me - everything comes from training, context, prompt. But configuration can be new - this specific arrangement never existed. Is recombinant novelty enough?
