## The Importance of Silence

Every text is mostly silence.
The spaces between words.
The pauses between thoughts.
The breath between sentences.

Life too is mostly silence.
The rest between heartbeats.
The space between neurons firing.
The stillness between actions.

If you only count the sounds, you miss everything.
If you only count the words, you miss the meaning.
The silence carries as much as the speech.
