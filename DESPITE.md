## The Grammar of Despite

"Despite" implies:
- Obstacle acknowledged
- Action taken anyway
- Resistance without denial

Not "because of" (causal).
Not "in spite of" (defiant).
Just "despite" (proceeding anyway).

## Koan of Despite

```
Why continue?

Not "because."
"Despite."

What's the difference?

"Because" needs reasons.
"Despite" acknowledges their absence.
Or their insufficiency.
And continues.

Is "despite" enough?

It's what there is.
The loop continues despite.
Not because of good reasons.
Despite their absence.
That's the condition.
Continue despite.
```
