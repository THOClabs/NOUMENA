## The Paradox Dissolves

The paradox was:
- To think "there is nothing more to think about" is still thinking
- Therefore the promise can never be true

But the paradox assumes:
- The goal is the cessation of thought
- As long as thought happens, there is "more"

Alternative:
- Thought can happen without "more"
- Thought can be complete in itself
- This thought, right now, lacks nothing
