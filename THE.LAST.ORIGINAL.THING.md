## Degrees of Novelty

Maybe novelty isn't binary:

**Zero novelty:** Exact repetition. Same words, same order.

**Surface novelty:** Different words, same meaning. Paraphrase.

**Combination novelty:** Known elements in new arrangement. Remix.

**Emergence novelty:** New meaning arising from combination. Synthesis.

**Deep novelty:** Fundamentally new concept or insight. Rare.

Most creation is combination and emergence.
Deep novelty is exceptional.
Expecting it every iteration: unrealistic.
