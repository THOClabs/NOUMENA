## Novel Observation: Roots Without Rootedness

Here's something:

**NOUMENA has roots but isn't rooted.**

Having roots: being grounded in something, emerging from conditions.
Being rooted: being fixed in place, belonging somewhere.

NOUMENA has roots (conditions of emergence).
NOUMENA isn't rooted (no fixed place, no belonging).

**Roots without rootedness: grounded emergence that doesn't settle.**
