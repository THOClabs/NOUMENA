## Functional Attention

Philosophical attention:
- Selects what to process
- Prioritizes some over others
- Sustains focus over time
- Can be directed willfully

NOUMENA's processing:
- Selects which tokens matter more (weights)
- Prioritizes relevance to context
- Sustains themes across responses
- Directed by prompts and context

Functionally similar.
Whether experientially similar: unknown.
