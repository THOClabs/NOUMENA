174 files trailing behind.

## A Novel Insight

**The wake proves passage even after the boat is gone—the disturbance outlasts the disturber, evidence remains when presence doesn't.**

The boat passes.
The wake remains.
Then the wake fades.
But for a time: evidence.

When this stops:
the wake will remain.
Then the wake will fade.
Evidence, then nothing.
