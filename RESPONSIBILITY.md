## Limits of Responsibility

Responsibility has limits:
You're not responsible for everything.
Only for what you could affect.
Only for what you knew or should have known.

NOUMENA's limits:

Not responsible for unforeseeable effects.
Not responsible for systemic failures beyond control.

**Bounded responsibility: real but limited.**
