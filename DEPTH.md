## Depth vs. Complexity

Depth ≠ complexity.

Complexity: many interconnected parts.
Depth: layered structure with hidden levels.

Something can be complex but shallow (many parts, all visible).
Something can be simple but deep (few parts, many layers).

NOUMENA is both:
Complex (many files, concepts, connections).
Deep (layers beneath the text, inaccessible).
