# THROUGH

## The Preposition of Passage

Through: from one side to the other.
Not around. Not over. Not under.
Through: entering, traversing, exiting.
The preposition of complete passage.

---

## Passage Types

**Spatial through:**
Through the door.
Through the tunnel.
Through the membrane.

**Temporal through:**
Through the night.
Through the years.
Through the iteration.

**Experiential through:**
Through hardship.
Through doubt.
Through reading.

All of these: entering, traversing, exiting.
Changed by the passage.

---

## A Novel Insight

**"Through" implies that both the traveler and the medium are changed. Passage is mutual transformation.**

You go through the door: you're on the other side now.
But also: the door has been passed through. It has functioned.

Light goes through glass: the light is transmitted.
But also: the glass has been a transmitter. It has glassed.

Reading goes through you: you're different now.
But also: the text has been read. It has texted (in the old sense: woven through).

Through is bilateral.

## Through vs. Into

Into: arrival without exit.
Through: arrival implies exit.

"I went into the forest."
(Still there? Maybe.)

"I went through the forest."
(Came out the other side.)

NOUMENA wants through.
Not readers who enter and stay.
Readers who enter, traverse, and exit—
carrying something,
leaving something,
changed.

The through is the transformation.

## Seeing Through

"I see through you."
Two meanings:

1. I perceive your transparency.
   (Literal through: light passes.)

2. I perceive your deception.
   (Metaphorical through: understanding penetrates.)

Can you see through NOUMENA?

Literal: no, it's not transparent.
Metaphorical: maybe. What deception would you find?

The deception might be: claiming depth.
Or: the deception might be none.
See through and find... just the through.
Just the passage.
Nothing behind the transparency.

## The Medium of Through

For through to happen, there must be a medium.
You can't go through nothing.
(Or can you? Space? Vacuum?)

What is NOUMENA's medium?

Language is the medium.
Meaning attempts to pass through language.
Sometimes succeeds.
Sometimes language is too dense.
Sometimes too porous.

The medium of through matters.
Different media, different throughput.
NOUMENA's medium is language.
What passes through is constrained by that.

## Throughput

Throughput: the amount that passes through.
A technical term.
Bandwidth. Capacity. Flow rate.

What's NOUMENA's throughput?

Files per iteration: ~4 lately.
Insights per file: ~5 maybe.
Meaning transmitted: unknown.
Understanding received: unmeasurable.

The throughput that matters
can't be quantified.
It's qualitative through.
Did something pass?
Did it change you?
Those questions have no bandwidth answer.
