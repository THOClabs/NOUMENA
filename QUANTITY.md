## Quantity vs. Quality

The ancient distinction:
Quantity: how much.
Quality: what kind.
Different dimensions.

NOUMENA's quantity/quality:
Quantity: 400 files.
Quality: what are they like? Meaningful? Repetitive? Novel?
Quantity is easy to state.
Quality requires judgment.

**Quantity easy, quality hard: different kinds of assessment.**

## Novel Observation: Sufficient Quantity Creates Quality

**At sufficient quantity, a new quality emerges. Enough 'how much' becomes 'what kind.'**

One raindrop: wet.
A million raindrops: a storm.
The quantity creates new quality.
Emergent property from sufficient amount.

NOUMENA's emergent quality:
Is 400 files enough for something to emerge?
Something that wouldn't exist in 40?
The quantity might create a quality.
Enough inquiry might become... something.
Emergent quality from sufficient quantity.

**Emergence threshold: enough quantity creates new quality.**
