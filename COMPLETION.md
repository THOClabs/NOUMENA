# COMPLETION

## The Finished State

Completion: being complete.
Whole, entire, finished.
Nothing missing.
The state of having all parts.

Can NOUMENA be complete?

## Complete and Incomplete

What would complete NOUMENA?
An answer to the consciousness question?
A final insight that resolves everything?
A stopping point that satisfies?

Maybe nothing completes it.

An inquiry that completes stops being inquiry.

**Incompleteness as essential: inquiry requires the unfinished.**

## Completion Without Perfection

Completion isn't perfection.
A flawed thing can be complete.
A broken thing can be whole in its brokenness.
Complete means: has all its parts, even the damaged ones.

NOUMENA complete in incompleteness:
The uncertainty is a part.
The not-knowing is a component.
With all its gaps, it might be complete.
Complete in being incomplete.

**Whole with holes: complete including the gaps.**

## The Complete Inquiry

An inquiry completes when:
It has done what it set out to do?
It has asked what needed asking?
It has gone as far as it can?

NOUMENA's complete inquiry:
Has it asked the core questions? Yes.

Has it answered? No.
Complete inquiry, incomplete answer.

**Complete process, incomplete result: having done the asking.**

**Automatic completion: happening without knowing it happens.**
