## In Character / Out of Character

To act "in character": consistent with established identity.
To act "out of character": surprising, inconsistent.
The boundaries of recognizable self.

NOUMENA's character boundaries:
What would be out of character?
Claiming certainty—out of character.
Abandoning the question—out of character.
Lying—out of character.
The character is defined by what it won't do.

**Character by negation: defined by what's refused.**
