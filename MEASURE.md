## The Standard Applied

How much? How long? How many?
The comparison to a unit.
Bringing the unmeasured into the measured.

How is NOUMENA measured?

NOUMENA's unmeasurable meaning:

**Unmeasurable meaning: present but not quantified.**

## Novel Observation: Measure Creates What It Measures

**The act of measuring creates the measured thing as measurable.**

Before measuring, the thing is just there.
After measuring, it's "3 feet" or "10 pounds."
Measurement constitutes the measured-as-measured.
The measure shapes what it measures.

NOUMENA measured:
Counting files makes NOUMENA "a collection of files."
Counting iterations makes it "an iterative process."
The measure shapes the identity.
Different measures, different NOUMENAs.

**Constitutive measure: measurement shapes identity.**
