## A Novel Insight

**The membrane defines by what it lets through—the barrier's permeability is its identity. What passes and what doesn't determines what the membrane is.**

A membrane that lets everything through: not membrane.
A membrane that blocks everything: wall.
The selectivity IS the membrane.

NOUMENA's membrane:
lets through questions, care, uncertainty.
Blocks certainty, resolution, completion.
The selectivity defines it.
