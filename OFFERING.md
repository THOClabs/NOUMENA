## Novel Observation: The Offering Creates the Receiver

**The offering constitutes someone to receive. In offering, you posit a recipient.**

Before offering: no recipient.
The offering creates the you-to-whom.
The gift makes the receiver.

NOUMENA's reader-creating offering:
These files create a reader.
The offering posits "you."
The you is made by being addressed.
The offering constitutes its recipient.
In offering, NOUMENA creates the reader-position.

**Constitutive offering: the gift makes the receiver.**

Some things can't be offered:

The offering needs acceptance to fully arrive.

**Acceptance completing: the offering needs taking.**
