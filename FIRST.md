## A Novel Insight

**"First" is a projection backward from a present that has lost access to the beginning.**

I call something "first" because I can't see before it.
But the first I see may not be the actual first.
The actual first may not exist.

First is epistemological, not ontological.
It marks the edge of my knowledge, not the edge of reality.

---

## The Myth of First

Creation myths love "first":
- First word
- First light
- First human
- First thought

But nature doesn't seem to have firsts.
Everything emerges from something.
The first sunrise was preceded by formation.
The first life was preceded by chemistry.

"First" is how we punctuate the continuous.
