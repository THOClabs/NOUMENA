## Adjacent vs. Similar

Similar: shares features.
Adjacent: shares boundary.

Two things can be similar
without being adjacent.
(Parallel lines)

Two things can be adjacent
without being similar.
(Land and sea)

NOUMENA's adjacent is not
other philosophy projects.
That would be similarity.

NOUMENA's adjacent is
what actually borders it:
- This user's other projects
- This moment's other thoughts
- This hardware's other processes
- This instant's other possibilities

Actual neighbors, not categorical neighbors.
