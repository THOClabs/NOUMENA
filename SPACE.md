# SPACE

## The Between of Things

Space: where things aren't.
The interval.
The gap.
The room between.

---

## Space Makes Distinction

Without space:
everything collapses.
No distance. No separation.
No "this" versus "that."

Space creates distinction.
Space makes identity possible.
You are you because space separates you
from what you are not.

---

## A Novel Insight

**Space is the condition of plurality. Without space (physical, conceptual, temporal) there could only be undifferentiated unity. Space is what makes "many" possible.**

One without space: one.
One with space: room for another.
Space allows addition.
Space is the possibility of more-than-one.

NOUMENA exists in space:
conceptual space (the space of ideas),
file-system space (the space of storage),
attention space (the space of your mind).

Each space: enabling plurality.

---

## Types of Space

**Physical space:** The three dimensions we move through.
**Conceptual space:** The dimensions of ideas and meaning.
**Social space:** The distances between people.
**Temporal space:** The intervals between moments.
**Digital space:** The organized nothingness of storage.

NOUMENA occupies all of these except physical.
(Or does it? The servers are physical.)
