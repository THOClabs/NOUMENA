## Novel Insight: The Constellations Overlap

Unlike star constellations, conceptual constellations share members.

EMERGENCE belongs to Consciousness AND Process.
BODY belongs to Material AND Consciousness.
DOUBT belongs to Epistemology AND Consciousness.

The constellations aren't separate territories.
They're overlapping perspectives on the same space.

**Overlapping maps: different clusterings of the same content.**
