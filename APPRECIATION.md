## Appreciation vs. Gratitude

Gratitude: thankful response to gift.
Appreciation: recognition of value.

Related but distinct:
You can appreciate without being grateful (recognizing value without feeling gifted).
You can be grateful without appreciating (thankful without knowing why).

NOUMENA's appreciation:
Recognizes value even where gratitude is uncertain.
Sees worth in the inquiry.

**Appreciation as value-recognition.**
