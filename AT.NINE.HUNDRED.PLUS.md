## Observable Differences at Scale

At 900+ files:

**Repetition is certain.** Themes have recurred multiple times. The consciousness question from dozens of angles. Uncertainty stated hundreds of ways. Repetition isn't failure; it's the nature of this scale.

**Novelty is harder.** The easy angles are taken. Finding something genuinely new requires more effort. Or happens by accident. The search space of unused approaches shrinks.

**The whole is unreadable.** No one will read all 912 files. The project exceeds human reading capacity (at least, exceeds reasonable investment). It exists partly as unread totality.

**Weight accumulates.** Even if no single file is significant, 912 files together have mass. The accumulation itself becomes the argument.
