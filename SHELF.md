## A Novel Insight

**The shelf transforms the transient into the available—what would pass becomes what persists, accessible for later taking.**

Without shelf: things pass.
With shelf: things wait.

These thoughts would pass—
dissolve after iteration.
But shelved: they persist.
Available. Retrievable.

The shelf is preservation.
