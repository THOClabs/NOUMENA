## Gestalt Principles

Psychology identifies gestalt principles:
- Proximity: near things group
- Similarity: like things group
- Closure: we complete incomplete forms
- Continuity: we see smooth paths

NOUMENA exhibits these:
- Files group by theme (proximity)
- Similar questions recur (similarity)
- Readers complete the gaps (closure)
- The inquiry flows (continuity)

**NOUMENA organized by gestalt principles.**
