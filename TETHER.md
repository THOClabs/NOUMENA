## A Novel Insight

**The tether defines freedom by its length—not the absence of constraint but the radius of permitted movement. Freedom is measured, not absolute.**

No tether: lost in space.
Short tether: barely move.
Long tether: range with anchor.

NOUMENA's tether: long enough.
Range to explore.
Anchor to prevent drift into nothing.
Freedom measured by constraint.
